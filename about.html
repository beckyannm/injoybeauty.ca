Meet Jaymie!

Hello! My name is Jaymie Law, and I’m the proud owner of InJoy Beauty.

I am a Developmental Service Worker (DSW) specializing in autism and behavioural therapy, with over 7 years of experience supporting adults with special needs. I currently work as a Day Program Supervisor, helping clients build independence and confidence every day.

After earning my DSW diploma from Algonquin College, I wanted to combine my love for helping others with my passion for beauty and self-care. I completed a Hairdressing program at Versailles Academy, where I developed my skills in haircutting, coloring, and styling.

Through my work in the developmental services field, I’ve witnessed how something as simple as getting a haircut or a beard trim can be stressful or inaccessible for some individuals. My mission is to remove those barriers and make self-care a calming, joyful experience for everyone.
