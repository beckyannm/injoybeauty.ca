Welcome to InJoy Beauty!

At InJoy Beauty, my goal is to make beauty services accessible, comfortable, and inclusive for everyone. I provide gentle, sensory-friendly beauty care for individuals who may experience challenges with traditional salon environments — including those with sensory sensitivities, mobility concerns, or anxiety.

Whether you prefer to have your services completed in your own home (extra charge applies for mobile services) or at my sensory-friendly, wheelchair-accessible home salon in Bourget, Ontario, you’ll be welcomed with understanding, patience, and care.

Everyone deserves to feel beautiful — in a space that feels safe.
